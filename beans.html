Beans

Beans are a type of legume, specifically the rootstock of the onion family,which is part of the cabbage family. They are usually green, dark brown,
or black in color, and are often eaten raw, cooked, or used in sauces and stews.

  Types of Beans
1. Kidney beans: Commonly used in chili recipes and salads.
2. Black beans: Often used in Latin American cuisine, such as in rice and beans or empanadas.
3. Chickpeas: Also known as garbanzo beans, they're commonly used in hummus and Middle Eastern cuisine.
4. Pinto beans: Often used in refried beans and other Mexican dishes.
5. Navy beans: Small, white beans commonly used in baked beans and stews.
6. Lima beans: Large, flat beans often used in soups and stews.
7. Cannellini beans: Large, white beans commonly used in Italian cuisine.
8. Great Northern beans: Large, white beans often used in baked beans and stews.
